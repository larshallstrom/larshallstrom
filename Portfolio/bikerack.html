Hardwood Double Bike Rack

Maple bike rack specifically designed to store a 29" mountain bike and road bike.

When moving into my house sophomore year I needed a way to store both of my bikes in a space efficient way. I designed this bike rack specifically to accommodate a mountain bike with 29” tires and a 60 cm road bike. I built the rack of completely of maple hardwood using hidden joints and many woodworking techniques.
